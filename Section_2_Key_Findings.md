## üìù Customization Tips

### For Different Industries
**E-commerce:** Focus on cart abandonment, shipping costs, product returns  
**SaaS:** Emphasize churn, trial conversions, feature adoption  
**Retail:** Highlight inventory turnover, foot traffic, seasonal trends  

### For Different Audiences
**C-Suite:** Lead with profit impact, timelines, ROI  
**Operations:** Emphasize process changes, resource needs  
**Sales Team:** Focus on actionable tactics, territory changes  

### Tone Adjustments
**Urgent/Crisis Mode:** Use stronger language ("Critical", "Immediate action required")  
**Opportunity Focused:** Emphasize upside ("Untapped potential", "Growth lever")  
**Conservative:** Include risk mitigation plans for each action
